Scenario 1: Product Launch Video Simple Prompt Create a promotional video for the launch of a new smartphone. Show the product, its modern design, and people using it in daily life. End with the product name and launch date.

Detailed Prompt Generate a 60-second cinematic product launch video for a new smartphone called "Nova X1." Begin with a dark background and dramatic lighting revealing the phone's sleek metallic design. Show close-up shots of the camera, display, and slim body. Include scenes of professionals using the phone for photography, video calls, gaming, and navigation. Add smooth camera movements, futuristic transitions, feature highlights, energetic background music, and conclude with the slogan "Nova X1 – The Future in Your Hands" along with the launch date and company logo.

Expected Output The simple prompt generates a basic product showcase. The detailed prompt generates a professional advertisement with enhanced visuals, transitions, and storytelling. Scenario 2: Anime Story Video Simple Prompt Create an anime-style video about a young hero who discovers magical powers and begins an adventure to save the world.

Detailed Prompt Generate a 90-second anime-style story video featuring a 16-year-old boy named Kai who discovers an ancient crystal in a hidden forest and gains lightning powers. Show his journey from an ordinary village life to becoming a hero who protects his kingdom from a dark force. Include anime-style visuals, training scenes, emotional interactions, dramatic battles, cinematic camera angles, visual effects, subtitles, and inspiring background music. End with Kai standing on a mountain at sunrise, hinting at future adventures.

Expected Output The simple prompt generates a straightforward anime story with minimal details. The detailed prompt generates a rich narrative with character development, visual effects, and cinematic storytelling. Result: The experiment demonstrates that prompt structure significantly influences the quality of AI-generated videos. Simple prompts produce basic outputs with limited detail, while detailed prompts generate more coherent, visually appealing, and engaging videos. Detailed prompts provide clearer instructions regarding scenes, characters, camera movements, visual effects, and storytelling, resulting in outputs that better match the intended scenario. Thus, the prompting techniques for AI video generation were successfully explored and analyzed using multiple prompt structures.
